About Salas 64

Salas 64 is created and run by Steven Salas, a 16-year-old from Pasadena, California. The rankings are updated every day during the season to track the top teams in the country. The goal is to give fans a clear, bracket-focused view of who’s rising, who’s slipping, and which teams you should trust when you build your March Madness bracket.
